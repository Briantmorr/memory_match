How to play:

Help the monkey collect all the fruit!
Use the buttons on the right side or the arrow keys to move the monkey!
Click on the tiles with the click button (or push enter) to see the cards underneath!
Do your best to match all the titles, but be careful, once you make a match the tiles will disappear.

If you try to move into a space that doesn't have a wooden block you will fall and lose a life!
Try to get all the fruit before you run out of lives!
If you can get 8 matches you win!
if you can get all 12 you are the king of the jungle!
